Open Source Manifesto — 29 March 2026

I believe in the freedom of Happiness.
Every day I use VS Code, which proves that when we share code openly,
the whole world benefits.

If I could build and share one thing freely, it would be Any Software.

Just like VLC Media Player — created by students so that no one
can ever lock video behind a paywall — I choose to stand for open,
transparent, and collaborative software.

— tars-k
